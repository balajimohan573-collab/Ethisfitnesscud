About Us

Your Safe Space
to Get Stronger

Ethis Fitness Centre is more than a gym — it's a movement. Located on Nethaji Road, Manjakkuppam, near Lena Medical, Cuddalore, we're designed exclusively for women. We combine physical training with a foundation of values, helping every member build strength, confidence, and balance in life.

"The gym is comfortable and safe for women. The trainer is polite, knowledgeable, and friendly. They give continuous positive encouragement and organise classes in a very structured manner." – Justdial Member Review

Our certified trainer provides personalised attention to every member — from correct posture guidance to customised workout plans — ensuring you see real results in a safe, comfortable, home-like environment.

We combine modern fitness equipment with physiotherapy expertise, helping you build strength, improve health, and regain confidence every single day.
